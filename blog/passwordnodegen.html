Password Generator In Node.JS

Created On June 23 2023

Modified on Jun 24 2023

Welcome to my first blog page! I apologize for the lack of updates over the past seven months, as my school's internet filter imposed limitations. But before I get ahead of myself, I want to discuss the password generator I created using Node.js. You can visit the webpage at genpasswordnode.onrender.com The purpose of this tool is to generate secure passwords within minutes, especially for those who struggle to come up with new passwords. I utilized EJS for generating the passwords and Express for the frontend, allowing password generation directly through HTML.

The image on the left displays the Character Set of the Password Generator. You can select whether you want uppercase or lowercase letters, symbols, or numbers, as well as customize the length of the password. If you're curious, when you generate a password, the backend verifies the checkboxes you selected and incorporates the characters into the randomizer. The randomizer selects a letter or character for each position in the password, and it utilizes the ejs package to output the final result. It took me approximately two days to develop this functionality.
